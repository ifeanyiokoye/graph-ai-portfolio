About the course

Generative AI for Work & Research is a hands-on, 8-week live cohort on no-code AI tools for task automation and research productivity. It combines weekly workshops, help sessions, and tailored feedback within a supportive community.

Weekly live workshops Help sessions Capstone coaching Personalized feedback Certificate of completion Lifetime tutorials access

Modules (highlights)

    Grading rubric: Quizzes 15% · Assignments 35% · Attendance & participation 10% · Final project 40%.

    Learning outcomes

    • Explain LLM concepts and apply prompt patterns for research tasks.
    • Automate data prep, analysis, and reporting with AI assistants.
    • Design custom GPTs/chatbots with retrieval over your corpus.
    • Build agentic workflows to reduce repetitive effort.
    • Create clear visuals, summaries, and outreach artifacts.
